Kat's History

Cultivated relationships with local vendors for participation in a nationwide study of food borne pathogens in food. Culturally confirmed samples and prepared positive isolates for long-term storage. Prepared media and reagents for use in aseptic sample processing. Conducted environmental monitoring and disposal of biohazardous materials (autoclave).
